Welcome to the Pokemon site

An anime series based on the popular Game Boy game "Pocket Monsters" in which children raise a pocket monster and train it to fight other monsters. In this show, Satoshi and his Pokemon, Pikachu, travel the land hoping to improve their skills and eventually become the grand champions.

Ash Ketchum

He is the protagonist of the Pokémon anime and certain manga series as well as on various merchandise related to the franchise

Misty

Gym Leader of Cerulean City's Gym, known officially as the Cerulean Gym. She gives the Cascade Badge to Trainers who defeat her in battle. She specializes in Water-type Pokémon.

Brock

Gym Leader of Pewter City's Gym, known officially as the Pewter Gym. He gives the Boulder Badge to Trainers who defeat him in battle. He specializes in Rock-type Pokémon.
